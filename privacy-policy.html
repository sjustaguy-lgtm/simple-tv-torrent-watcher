Privacy Policy for Simple TV Torrent Watcher

Effective Date: May 22, 2026

Simple TV Torrent Watcher is committed to providing a secure, transparent, and localized user experience. This Privacy Policy outlines our data management principles and practices.

1. Data Collection and Transmission

Simple TV Torrent Watcher operates as a local-first browser extension.

  • No Developer Collection: The developer does not collect, harvest, store, sell, or receive personally identifiable information, browsing history, user credentials, IP addresses, watchlists, or torrent-client settings.
  • Local Storage: Application configuration settings, TV watchlists, optional torrent-client connection settings, and episode tracking strings are saved inside your browser profile using the chrome.storage.local API. This data is stored on your device and is not accessible to the developer.

2. Third-Party API Interactions

To deliver its core features, the extension interacts directly with public endpoints and user-configured local services through network requests initiated from your browser:

  • TVMaze API: Title resolution queries may be sent to public TVMaze metadata endpoints to cross-reference show title text. No user-specific tokens are sent during these lookups.
  • EZTV Page/API Access: The extension reads supported EZTV pages and related public endpoint responses to compare visible release information against your local watchlist. The extension does not upload analytics or tracking data from these actions.
  • User-Configured RSS Feeds: If the user adds an RSS feed URL, the extension may request that feed directly from the browser to compare release titles against the local watchlist. RSS feed addresses are stored locally and are not sent to the developer.
  • Local Client RPC/WebUI: When configured by the user, selected magnet links may be sent directly to the user's own torrent client WebUI/RPC endpoint over localhost or a local/private network address. These requests are sent directly from the browser to the user-configured client.

3. Chrome Web Store Limited Use Compliance

Simple TV Torrent Watcher is designed to comply with Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies.

The extension does not sell user data, transfer user data to advertising platforms, transfer user data to data brokers, or use user data to evaluate creditworthiness.

User data handled by the extension is limited to supporting the extension's single purpose: maintaining a local TV watchlist, checking supported or user-configured metadata/release sources, and sending user-selected magnet links to a user-configured torrent client.

4. Changes to This Policy

Updates or adjustments to this policy may be included with future extension updates or published on the extension's public listing or project page.

5. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the contact channel listed on the extension's distribution marketplace page or project repository.
