Simple TV Torrent Watcher

Version 2.0.4. A local-first browser extension written for Brave and usable on Chromium-based browsers.

Install from Chrome Web Store Privacy Policy GitHub Repository

What It Does

Simple TV Torrent Watcher adds a watchlist and new-episode scanner to supported EZTV pages, can check user-added RSS feeds, and can send selected magnet links to a supported torrent client WebUI or to the local torrent app on the same computer.

  • Adds Scan for New Episodes, Watchlist, Add Show to Watchlist, and Settings buttons on supported EZTV pages.
  • Version 2.0.4 removes uploader/release group suffixes such as -MeGusta[eztvx.to] from saved show names when importing pasted filenames.
  • Version 2.0.3 checks built-in EZTV RSS when the EZTV JSON API lags behind.
  • Parses RSS magnet links from torrent:magnetURI entries.
  • Keeps the 2.0.2 Settings fix, opening Settings in a new extension tab to avoid Brave page/client blocking.
  • Keeps your show watchlist in browser storage.
  • Imports and exports backup lines like M.I.A | S01E09.
  • Imports qBittorrent/EZTV filenames like Show.Name.S02E06.1080p.HEVC.x265-MeGusta[eztvx.to].
  • Supports whole-season requests like FROM S02E00.
  • Checks EZTV, TVMaze, and optional user-added RSS feeds.

Supported Torrent Clients

Direct WebUI/RPC support is included for qBittorrent, Transmission, Deluge, uTorrent, BitTorrent, and aria2. You can also use same-computer magnet mode if you want your browser to open magnets in the local torrent app.

Best Ways To Add A Show

Paste into Add Show to Watchlist or the bulk import box. Codec words such as HEVC and x265 are ignored when figuring out the show name. Quality words such as 1080p are kept as that show's first quality choice.

What you typeWhat it does
FROMAdds the show and tracks it from now on.
FROM s02Adds season 2 and starts from S02E00, so episode 1 and newer in season 2 can be found.
FROM S02E00Useful when you want the whole season.
FROM s02e05Marks episode 5 as already handled, so scans look for newer season 2 episodes.
FROM 2x05Same as S02E05 using the alternate episode format.
FROM season 2Adds the whole season starting from S02E00.
FROM season 2 episode 5Same as S02E05.
FROM 1080p s03Adds season 3 starting from S03E00 and prefers 1080p first.
M.I.A | S01E09Import/export format; saves M.I.A with S01E09 as the last episode already handled.

Tip: use S02E00 when you want the whole season. Use S02E05 when you already have episode 5 and only want newer episodes.

Privacy

The extension is local-first. Watchlists, settings, custom RSS feed addresses, and torrent-client connection settings are stored in your browser profile using browser storage.

The developer does not collect, receive, sell, or store your watchlist, browsing history, torrent-client settings, credentials, IP address, or analytics.
